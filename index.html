FINE FOODS

FineFoods (Food Delivery App) – Customer & Partner Apps

As the lead mobile developer, I built and maintained both the customer app and the partner app for FineFoods, available on Android and iOS using Flutter. The customer app allowed users to browse restaurants, place orders, track deliveries, and make payments seamlessly. The partner app enabled restaurant owners to manage their menus, track orders, and communicate with customers in real time. I ensured smooth API integration, a responsive UI/UX, and efficient state management for optimal performance.

SternPay

Fintech Mobile App

SternPay is a fintech mobile application designed to provide seamless and secure digital payment solutions for users. As the lead Flutter developer, I was responsible for building and optimizing the app for both Android and iOS, ensuring a smooth user experience. Key Features: Instant Money Transfers – Users can send and receive payments quickly and securely. Bill Payments – Integrated functionality for paying utility bills, subscriptions, and other services. Wallet & Transaction Management – Users can track their transactions, manage their wallets, and view detailed payment history. Security & Authentication – Implemented biometric authentication, OTP verification, and encryption to ensure secure transactions. API Integration – Seamless connection with banking APIs and third-party financial services. Modern UI/UX – Designed an intuitive and user-friendly interface for effortless navigation. I utilized Flutter, Firebase, RESTful APIs, and state management solutions to build a reliable and high-performance fintech solution. SternPay empowers users with fast, secure, and hassle-free digital transactions, contributing to the growth of cashless payments in modern financial ecosystems. 🚀

FINELOOKS

Beauty & Wellness Service Marketplace

FineLooks is a comprehensive mobile platform that connects customers with beauty and wellness service providers. As the lead mobile developer, I built and maintained both the FineFoods customer app and the partner app for Android and iOS using Flutter. Key Features: Customer App: Browse and book beauty and wellness services. Seamless payment integration for online transactions. Real-time service tracking and appointment reminders. User-friendly UI with personalized recommendations. Partner App: Service providers can manage bookings and schedules. Business performance analytics and earnings tracking. Push notifications for new appointments and customer requests. Secure verification and authentication for professionals. By leveraging Flutter, Firebase, and RESTful APIs, I ensured a smooth, high-performance, and scalable experience for both customers and service providers. FineLooks revolutionizes how users access beauty and wellness services, offering a seamless, digital-first approach to self-care and professional engagement. 🚀
